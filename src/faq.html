FAQ

Have a question? We'll answer it.

Why doesn't [insert product name] show up when I search for it?

Some products are region-locked and cannot be accessed from outside Japan.

Due to the backend server's geographic location outside Japan, some products may be excluded from search results and not displayed. Alternatively, localized versions of those products in languages other than Japanese may be displayed.

Where do you get the product data from?

From the official LINE Store website, via a backend proxy.

What about UI localization?

It's a pain, so I don't want to do it. If you're not an alien, you should be able to read it using your browser's translation feature.

Is this project open source?

Yes, the source code for this website and backend proxy is available on GitHub. If you're a developer, please feel free to contribute!

Why are you putting up such sh*tty ads?

I dislike ads, but server operating costs and various infrastructure management expenses are not cheap. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding.
